Privacy Policy

Last updated:

This Privacy Policy explains how Fyndr (“we”, “our”, “us”) collects, uses, and protects information when you use the Fyndr mobile application and related services.

Information we may collect

  • Account data: name, email/phone (if you create an account)
  • Profile data: optional profile photo and preferences
  • Location data: approximate or precise location if you grant permission (used for nearby events)
  • App activity: events you create/join, chats, ratings, and feedback
  • Device data: device identifiers and push notification tokens (for notifications)

How we use information

  • Provide core features (events, chat, invites, rating)
  • Show relevant events and improve recommendations
  • Send notifications you request/enable
  • Prevent abuse, enforce policies, and improve security

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited data with service providers (for example, hosting, analytics, or push notifications) strictly to operate the app.

Data retention

We retain data only as long as needed to provide the service and comply with legal obligations. You can request deletion (see Delete Account page).

Your choices

  • You can update your profile information inside the app.
  • You can disable location permissions or notifications in device settings.
  • You can request account and data deletion (see Delete Account page).
