Delete your Fyndr account

This page lets you request deletion even if you removed the app.

In-app deletion (recommended)

  1. Open Fyndr
  2. Go to Settings → Account
  3. Select Delete account and follow the prompts

Request deletion via email

Email us from the address/phone linked to your account:

muhy06@gmail.com

Subject suggestion: Delete my Fyndr account

What gets deleted

  • Your account profile and identifiers
  • Your saved preferences and device token
  • Associated app data that can be removed safely

What may be retained

  • Data required for legal compliance, fraud prevention, or security
  • Aggregated/anonymous analytics that can’t identify you

We may request verification to prevent unauthorized deletion.
